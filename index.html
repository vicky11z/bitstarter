The Youtube for sports. Take a video of yourself, your siblings, your friends, your coach; upload that video to YouNique. YouNique's community of sports players have the opportunity to watch, analyze, and critique YOUR technique.Whether your sport of choice be tennis, baseball, golf, even basketball or swimming, you will find a home amongst YouNique. Build off constructive criticism; watch other players; converse with people just as invested in the sport as you are. Improve your technique - it's simple. Just Upload, Analyze, Critique, and Improve.

With our video overlay feature, overlay your video with a video pulled from another source. In this way, you'll be able to compare your own technique with the movements of another player, whether it be a pro or a peer. View videos frame by frame, in slow motion, and play the two at the same time. Cut videos to focus on a specific instance; elongate them and watch an entire game. Piece apart the intricacies of a stroke you'd like to follow, a motion you'd like to emulate. It's easy: just Upload, Analyze, Critique, and Improve.
